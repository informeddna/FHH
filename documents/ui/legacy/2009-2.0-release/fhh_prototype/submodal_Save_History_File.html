Save My Family Health History
Your information is not saved on this web site or any other system.  You must save your family health history to your computer or portable disk (i.e. floppy disk, USB flash drive, etc.).  Your information will be saved as a file in XML format with a ".xml" file extension. 

After saving your family health history you will be able to:
     -- Continue to modify your information
     -- View a printable Pedigree diagram (your family tree highlighting diseases & conditions in your family history you select)
     -- View a printable Pedigree chart listing information entered into your family health history

You will use the saved file to re-load your information any time you leave the site and wish to come back. Otherwise, you would have to re-create your family history from scratch every time you visit.
