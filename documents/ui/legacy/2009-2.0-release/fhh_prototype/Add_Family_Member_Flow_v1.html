CURRENT FHH PROTOTYPE FLOW - ADD FAMILY MEMBER (v1)

USE CASE FLOW:
1. In the current FHH prototype flow the user begins by clicking "Add Family Member" on the My Family History page.
2. The system displays the submodal with a form to add an individual family member.
3. The user selects the family member they wish to add (based on that person's relationship to the user/proband). 
          -- IF the user selects a family member other than Brother, Sister, Son, or Daughter the system displays fields to determine the specific relationship to the user/proband, such as Side of Family and/or which family member this person is directly related to from those they have already entered into their history.

PROS & CONS

PROS:
  -- connections between family members is forced.  no "floaters" allowed.
  -- simple, straight-forward entry form
  -- 3 clicks from start to finish with the new family member's personal profile & health history entered.

CONS:
  -- family members are added one at a time, thus could be time-consuming for users with large family histories
  -- forces user to enter at least Living/Deceased information under family member's personal profile
  -- does not force users to declare total number of immediate family members (siblings, children, grand parents), thus allows for gaps within the overall family history.
  -- does not allow user to add a family members with a designation of "history unknown", which would prevent gaps in the overall family history.
