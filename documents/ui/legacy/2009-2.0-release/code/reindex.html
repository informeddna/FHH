Recenter My Family History on another Family Member

[DRAFT] You may make any relative be the center person of the Health History. You can then add relatives to create a new family health history without having to start from the very beginning.

:
[DRAFT] You can see the changes in a family tree diagram by choosing the 'Preview History' button. The changes will not be made until you click on the 'Recenter and Save History' button. This will replace the current Family Health History. Only family members that are a direct blood relative will display.
