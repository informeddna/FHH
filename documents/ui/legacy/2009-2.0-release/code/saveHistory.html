Save My Family History to My Computer

To ensure privacy of your personal health information, your information is not saved on this web site or any other system. You must save your family health history to your computer or portable disk (i.e. floppy disk, USB flash drive, etc.).

There are two ways to save your family history file:

  • Save as Self: Keeps you at the center of your family tree. It is highly recommended that you first save your file as Self, prior to saving it as another family member.
  • Save as Family Member: Creates a health history centered on one of your relatives instead of yourself. You can then give the health history to that relative so they can create their own family health history without having to start from the very beginning.
