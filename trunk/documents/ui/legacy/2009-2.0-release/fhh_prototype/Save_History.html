Save Family History File
To ensure privacy of your personal health information, your information is not saved on this web site or any other system.  You must save your family health history to your computer or portable disk (i.e. floppy disk, USB flash drive, etc.).  Your information will be saved as a file in XML format with a ".xml" file extension.  You will use the saved file to re-load your information any time you leave the site and wish to come back. Otherwise, you would have to re-create your family history from scratch every time you visit.
