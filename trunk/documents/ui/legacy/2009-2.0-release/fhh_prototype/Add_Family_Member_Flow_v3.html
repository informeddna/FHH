PROPOSED FHH PROTOTYPE FLOW - ADD FAMILY MEMBER (v3)

USE CASE FLOW:
For a user creating a health history from scratch...
1. The user begins by clicking "Start With Me" in the family tree on the My Family Health History page.
2. The system displays the "Edit ME" submodal with a form to enter the proband's personal profile & health history.  The user enters their information and clicks "Save".
3. The system displays the "Add Immediate Family Members" submodal with a form to add all immediate family members, including siblings, children, aunts, uncles, and grandparents of the proband.
4. The user enters the number for each family member and indicates whether or not to add grandparents and clicks "Build My Family Tree".
5. Ths system displays the My Family Health History page with an updated family tree showing the newly added family members.
6. The user may opt to add more family members by clicking "Add Family Members".
7. The system displays the "Add Individual Family Members" submodal with a form to add an idividual family member.
8. The user selects the family member they wish to add (based on that person's relationship to the user/proband). 
          -- IF the user selects a family member other than Brother, Sister, Son, or Daughter the system displays fields to determine the specific relationship to the user/proband, such as Side of Family and/or which family member this person is directly related to from those they have already entered into their history.
9. The user may either "Save & Add Another" or "Save & Enter Information".  IF "Save & Enter Information"...
10. The system displays the "Edit Family Member" submodal to edit the selected family member's personal profile & health history.

PROS & CONS

PROS:
  -- connections between family members is forced.  no "floaters" allowed.
  -- simple, straight-forward entry forms.
  -- allows for quick entry of immediate family members after the proband has created their profile.
  -- allows for quick entry of multiple family members with "save & add another".
  -- allows users to declare total number of immediate family members (siblings, children, grand parents), thus helping prevent gaps within the overall family history.
  -- 3 clicks from start to finish from "Start With Me" to entering proband profile, adding immediate family members and viewing updated family tree

CONS:
  -- Does not allow user to return to enter multiple family members at one time; however, this could be confusing for the user & for system logic -- for example, the user initially enters that they have 2 brothers and later returns to the same "Add Immediate Family Members" submodal and enters 3 brothers.  Does this mean they have a total of 3 brothers or a total of 5 brothers?  And what about the information they may have already entered for the 2 brothers initially entered, would that get overwritten?  Hence, a decision was made by the development team at this time to not allow users to return to the "Add Immediate Family Members" submodal for these reasons.

ADDITIONAL NOTES

Add Family Member from within "Edit Family Member" submodal
Another option for adding family members is to allow users to enter information from within the "Edit Family Member" submodal.  For example, if the user clicks on their Uncle from within the family tree, the submodal would include a question under the Personal Information section such as "How many sons and daughters does this uncle have?".  This would allow for quick development of the family tree and provide users a way to enter multiple family members at one time after having created their immediate family.  Upon further discussion of this opion with the development team it was decided that, due to project time constraints, this option would be explored further & implemented only if usability testing proves a clear need for it and the client requests and prioritizes it accordingly.
