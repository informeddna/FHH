Health care providers: Learn how My Family Health Portrait has been designed to improve the health of all Americans..
My Family Health Portrait is compatible with most browsers and operating systems. Please see our compatibility statement for more information. For users who prefer alternative versions of My Family Health Portrait or would like to collect information from family members in advance of filling out their family health history, a hard copy paper form is available.
Using My Family Health Portrait you can:

     1.   Enter your family health history.
     2.   Create drawings of your family health history to share with family or doctors.
     3.   Use the health history of your family to create your own.

Talking with your doctor about your family health history can help you stay healthy!
